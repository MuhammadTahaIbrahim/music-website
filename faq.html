Manage My Account and Profile

Signing up on Gaana is easy and simple, it just takes less than a minute, Open Gaana app menu and tap on the ''Login or register'' option at the top. Now, you can sign in via Facebook, Google, Email or mobile number. For Email and Phone sign up, just enter the required details on the sign up page. We will keep your details always in our locker :)

You can edit or change your name only from Gaana application. Follow these steps- Open the Gaana app Go to Gaana menu and tap on your current profile image Tap on edit profile icon Change your name and tap on the tick icon at top right corner There you go with the corrections!

If you have successfully linked your phone number with your email id on Gaana then you can login with either of them. You will be able to access your playlists, downloads and favorites even if you login with your mobile number.

You can edit your Gaana password from Gaana app - Open Gaana Menu Tap on "Login or Register" icon Select "Email Login" option and enter your registered email id Tap on "Forgot Password" A reset password link will be send to your registered email id You can create a new password using that link.
